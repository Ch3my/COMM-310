How did it all begin?

Photography started just as a hobby when a friend asked me to take some photos for his marriage. Until that moment I have never took pictures for an occasion like that. In that oportunity everything turned out well despite not having experience.

After that I realized I liked to take pictures. A short time before I had started taking pictures of important moments in life. Like birthdays, family trips, etc. Just to keep the memory. I started to study photography basics and now I take photos everytime I have the opportunity.

Jose "Chemy" Concha
